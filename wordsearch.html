Reduplication Dictionary

The Reduplication Dictionary is meant to help learners find reduplicated forms of words easily. You can search by English word or O’odham word. If you already know the reduplicated word, you can type it in and find its unreduplicated form and meaning.
Some words may be tagged by dialect. If a word has multiple entries across different dialects, you can use the drop down menu to narrow your search. If you choose a dialect from the drop down menu without searching a word, all the words that are tagged with that dialect will appear..
